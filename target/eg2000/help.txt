Building for the EACA EG2000 Colour Genie
=========================================

You will need:

* z88dk https://z88dk.org/

To build on a physical machine, call:

 make TARGET=eg2000 OPTION=assembly (Z80 assembly)
                    OPTION=default  (C)

Target files built at:

 eg2000/orterforth.cmd
 eg2000/orterforth.cas
 eg2000/orterforth.wav

To load and run, call:

 make run TARGET=eg2000
